A collaboration

Album Release

It's done. It's out. Treat your ears.

Listen now at Bandcamp.


The album is a collaboration between Chris Anderson of Firesuite, Arian Malekpour of Fly on Byrd, fly on & Hidden Mothers, Gareth Hughes of inFictions & Flatlands, Caroline Cawley of Dystopian Future Movies & Church of the Cosmic Skull, John Sephton, Nic Bowden - with additional vocals from Craig B of A Mote Of Dust, Aereogramme & The Unwinding Hours.

The album was pieced together at Old Pig Farm Studios high up in the hills of Sheffield over a long winter, and is a love letter to alternative pop, early shoegaze, HUGE choruses & dead pets.

And it was mastered by Bowling Green Studios who did '+/-' by Mew, so you know it's pro.

We hope you love it as much as we do

Album Recording/Live

So it's been a while since we had an update on here. We're still a band. Taz is still in Awooga and who are doing very well for themselves. Ari had a baby and seems surprised at how much time that takes up. Chris goes to the cinema a lot, stands in long queues on Record Store Day and I think has fled to Catalonia. Nic is still an enigma, not helped by the fact he's started speaking Italian almost exclusively at practice. Gareth built himself a bass from the corpses of several trees. And inbetween all this, we've had ooh.... 8 or so practices.

But I guess the main news is that we're playing live, so if you're wondering what the hell we've been up to this past year then:-

  • Me too now you mention it.
  • Come down to West Street Live, Sheffield on July 21st - Facebook events page link - to watch a bunch of great bands FOR FREE. We'll do a little band showcase on here in the run up to the gig so you can get hyped as fluff to brave the Tramlines human traffic to see us. There's a very sexy poster below by the talented Wou Wou and the Wormling (link to Twitter)

We've also spent some time with Tom Henthorn at Ebb Tone Recording Studios recording more tracks for an album which is now recorded and mixed, but nothing else yet. More updates on that as and when they happen.
