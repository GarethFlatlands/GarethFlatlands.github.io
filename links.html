What we love

Bands
  • Aereogramme - Scottish loveliness. Former band of Craig B who provided backing vocals on Hours. A handful of live releases are on this page but their proper discography is out there and well worth tracking down. (Bandcamp page)
  • Church of the Cosmic Skull - One of Caroline's other bands. New album "Science Fiction" available to buy. (Bandcamp page)
  • Efterklang - Hands up; I don't get it. But Ari and Chris love them. (Bandcamp page)
  • Firesuite - Chris' previous band. (Bandcamp page)
  • Fly on Byrd Fly On - Aris' previous band. (Bandcamp page)
  • inFictions - Gareth's been a lot of bands over the years. In the interests of not spamming this page with links to all of them, this is a good one, and current as a new album is on it's way after a 6 year break. (Bandcamp page)
  • Mew - Everyone in the band loves Mew, except Nic who nearly got kicked out when we found out he didn't know them. A big influence on us musically, and the reason Gareth went on holiday to Copenhagen.
  • Sigur Ros - No introduction needed, especially if you've seen a BBC nature documentary in the past decade. Songs that aren't 'Hoppipolla' are also available.
Places
  • The Old Pig Farm - Recording studio where we recorded everything.
  • The Bowling Green Studio - Everything we've done so far has been mastered by Dean Honer at The Bowling Green (Twitter Page)
